Friendlies and opening-weekend fixtures reflect officially published/reported matches as of 12 Jul 2026; the league table shows every club level on zero ahead of kickoff. This isn't every friendly every club is playing — only the ones publicly confirmed at build time — and later matchweeks will be added the same way once they're officially confirmed, rather than guessed in advance. Predictions are analysis written in advance from last season's final standings and known squad/manager changes, gated to reveal 7 days before each kickoff — they are not generated live by an AI model watching real-time stats, since a static page can't safely call one on its own; refreshing them with up-to-date research is something that has to be requested again as the season goes on. The MVP section reflects real match reports found at build time, not a live feed — two clubs' entries are their last competitive 2025/26 match because no 2026 pre-season friendly result could be confirmed as played yet, and several others honestly say "no standout named" where match reports didn't single anyone out, rather than inventing a name. The visit counter is a real, live count from a third-party counting API, not a made-up figure. League fixtures and scores are also overlaid with a real live-scores feed (API-Football), refreshed automatically roughly every 20 minutes by a scheduled job — if that feed is ever unreachable, the page falls back to the officially published schedule above and says so honestly rather than guessing a score. Always confirm kickoff times with official sources, as these can move for TV selection. This page only links out to official, legal broadcast partners.
